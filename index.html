How to Play
  1. Add 2-14 players and pick a player pool
  2. Take turns in a snake draft, passing the phone
  3. Each pick: choose a roster slot, search for an NFL player, then guess a season year (2000-2024)
  4. Points are scored by that player's real PPR stats that year — but you won't see the score until after you lock in your pick!
  5. Pick the wrong year and you get 0 points
  6. After each round, a leaderboard shows the standings
  7. Most total PPR points wins. Ties go to sudden death.
PPR Scoring
Passing: 1pt per 25 pass yds, 4pt per pass TD, -2pt per INT
Rushing: 1pt per 10 rush yds, 6pt per rush TD
Receiving: 1pt per reception, 1pt per 10 rec yds, 6pt per rec TD
